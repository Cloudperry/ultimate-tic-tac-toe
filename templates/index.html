Rules

  • The global board is a 3 x 3 grid of local boards . They are just like normal tic-tac-toe boards.
  • Placing marks/tokens
    • The first player can place a mark anywhere on the global board
    • After that players have to place their mark on a local board that corresponds to the local board position the previous mark was placed on. For example if the previous player places a mark in the upper left corner, the next player has to place their mark on the local board that is in the upper left corner of the global board.
  • Scoring/winning the game
    • Local boards can be won by a player just like a tic-tac-toe game is won. A local board that has been won will be marked by the player's mark who has won the board.
    • If a player gets "sent" to a local board that is won or filled, they can place a mark in any free cell
    • The game is won by winning 3 local boards in a horizontal, vertical or diagonal line
